# Pizza Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover insights about customer preferences, sales trends, and revenue patterns. 
The goal is to help a pizza business make data-driven decisions on menu offerings, promotions, and inventory management.

## Business Problem
Pizza restaurants often face challenges such as:
1. Identifying which pizza types are most popular.
2. Understanding peak sales periods.

   
This analysis addresses these questions using historical sales data.

## Skills
1. SQL: For querying, aggregating, and analyzing pizza sales data.
2. Power BI: For creating interactive dashboards and visualizing sales trends, revenue, and top-selling pizzas.

## Analysis Steps

- Data Cleaning: Remove duplicates, handle missing values, convert data types.
  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
   1. % sales by pizza category and size
   2. Revenue trends by month/day
   3. Total pizza sold by pizza type

- Insights Generation: Identify best-selling pizzas, lowest selling items, and revenue patterns.

- Visualization: Create charts and dashboards to present findings.

## Key Insights

- Most Popular Pizza: The Thai Chicken pizza accounts for 5.31% of total sales.

- Busiest days: Orders are highest on weekends, Fridays & Saturday evenings

- Most Popular Pizza by Category: Classic category contribute maximum sales and & total orders

- Most Popular Pizza by Size: Large size were the top sales driver
